The Grunkletoe Privacy Policy

A basic detail of information collected on you and how its processed.

Information grunklemoe stores

Grunklemoe stores player data from certain games, like burrito bison, fireboy and watergirl, papa's games, and more. This is just a basic save file. Grunklemoe doesn't store any more information than that. Unity games may process your information differently; see the bottom paragraph for more info.

How your information is handled

Grunklemoe does not share your information with anybody, the only time information is used is when you have a previous save file that is loaded.

Accounts on Henderson Clicker and the Grunklemoe Casino

These games use unity cloud solutions for its account management.
